My Sportsbike

What a beauty

I used my motorcycle for approximately 1.5 years before selling it at the end of 2019. I clocked approximately 15,000km.

Specifications of the 2018 Yamaha YZF-R6

  • Country of Manufacture: Japan
  • Year: 2018
  • Make: Yamaha
  • Model: YZF-R6
  • Color: Matte Grey
  • Power: 87.1 kW
  • Torque: 61.7 Nm
  • Weight: 190 kg

The engine is very reliable. For example, I rode it for hours on end, during 2 different weather extremes (blazing summer, chilling winter). Riding it was an incredible experience and I would encourage anybody to try it at least once in their lifetime!
